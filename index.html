OUR MISSION

Welcome to Montgomery, Alabama—GumpTown, where creativity ignites. GumpTown Records is more than music—we're a revolution. Built in the soul of our community, we amplify Black voices, cultivate undiscovered genius, and push urban sound to its limits.

From country to soul, conscious rap to R&B, we produce music that reflects our lived experiences, celebrates our heritage, and shapes the sound of tomorrow.
