OUR MISSION

GumpTown Records is more than a label—we're a cultural movement. Founded in the heart of the community, we're dedicated to amplifying Black voices, nurturing raw talent, and pushing the boundaries of urban music.

From trap to soul, conscious rap to R&B, we produce music that reflects our lived experiences, celebrates our heritage, and shapes the sound of tomorrow.
